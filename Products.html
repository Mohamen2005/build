Roca Rimless®

Rimless bowls are free of angles or nooks for accessible and more efficient cleaning. Water is propelled by a novel distribution system that spreads it homogeneously; the rimmed channel has been replaced by simplicity and hygiene without compromises.


Easy cleaning

Inner walls without angles or nooks where dirt might build up, making the cleaning of the WC easier.


Hygiene

Rimless toilets have a new water distribution system that propels water with greater force and spreads it homogeneously along the whole surface, exceeding EU standards.


Splash-free

Slight edge on the upper part of the bowl that prevents water from exceeding the limits of the piece, avoiding any splashing.
